Fretex

Fretex is the largest second-hand chain store in Norway. There is a total of 50 second-hand stores in Norway, 1 online store and 1 outlet store, operated by Fretex in Norway. Fretex is also the largest collector of used clothes and textiles in Norway, where they sell their collected clothes in stores or export to other customers in Europe , Pakistan and Iraq. Some of these will further export clothes and textiles to Asia and Africa.
