NMS Gjenbruk

NMS Gjenbruk is a store a little away from the noise from downtown. Is driven by NMS (Det Norske Misjonsselskap), a charity organisation, rooted in the Norwegian Church. Here you can find furniture, lamps, mirrors and artwork, in addition to kitchenware, toys and sport equipment.

Fretex

Fretex is the largest second-hand chain store in Norway. There is a total of 50 second-hand stores in Norway, 1 online store and 1 outlet store, operated by Fretex in Norway. In addition to being the largest collector of used clothes and textiles in Norway, they also have a huge collection of furniture items. The store is perfect for someone who is looking for something specific, practical or inexpensive.
