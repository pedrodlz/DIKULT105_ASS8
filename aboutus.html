About us

We are three students of University of Bergen trying to survive a course in Digital Culture in addition to help people in Bergen to live a greener life. To do both we created this site to make people aware of upcycling and present some of the stores in Bergen that provide you of second-hand items. Upcycling can be done in several ways, thus we created three main categories to make it easier to find information of your favour.

We would love to get feedback from you! If you have any questions, please send a mail to: info@upcyclingbergen.com.
